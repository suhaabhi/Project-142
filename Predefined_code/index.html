AI PING PONG







INSTRUCTIONS

  1. 1. First keep your laptop screen straight.
  2. 2. Move yourself approximately 3-4 feet away from the laptop.
  3. 3. Move your right wrist in an ascending and decending manner, a red dot should appear on your right wrist.
  4. 4. Now press the play button, and again move yourself 3-4 feet away from laptop.
  5. 5. The red paddel is your paddel, that will move as per the movement of your right wrist.
